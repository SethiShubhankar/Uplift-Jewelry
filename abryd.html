With a goal of achieving a greater social impact, Uplift Jewelry collaborates
with Abryd, a non-profit organization which aims to improve the quality of
education of children in rural Morocco.

Construction for their most recent project, the building of a primary school
in a rural village named ...,, will begin March 1st 2021 and is estimated to be
completed by June 1st 2021.

In collaboration with Abryd, the profits collected from the sale of Uplift
Jewelry rings from November 2020 to May 2021 will be used for the furnishing
of the newly built school. Profits will be used to purchase desks, to furnish the
teacher's room, any other classroom necessities and perhaps also to build a
sports or playground area.
