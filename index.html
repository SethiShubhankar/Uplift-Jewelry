Initially designed as a non-profit social project, Uplift Jewelry aims to empower, help and positively impact those less fortunate, by firstly supporting local craftmanship but also by using profits to develop social projects in rural communities, ultimately "uplifting" them.

- Melina Boyer

M I S S I O N   S T A T E M E N T
Uplift Jewelry first was established as a social initiative to raise profits for the renovation and refurnishing of a schools in the Atlas, in collaboration with Abryd Morocco.
