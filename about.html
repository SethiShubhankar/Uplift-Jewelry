Melina Boyer is a second year student in the International Development and Globalization Bachelors Degree at the University of Ottawa.


Passionate about social issues concerned with women's rights, conflict and inequality, Melina strives to undertake a career in Social Entrepreneurship and Documentary Photography.


Melina Boyer first started creating handmade silver rings, while in lockdown in Montreal in June 2020. Eager to achieve a greater social impact out of her passion for jewelry, she started building Uplift Jewelry when arriving back to Morocco in August 2020.
